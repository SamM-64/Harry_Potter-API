Hogwarts Legacy is an upcoming action role-playing video game that is set in the Harry Potter universe. It is developed by Avalanche Software and published by Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment. The game is set in the 1800s, long before the events of the Harry Potter series, and players will take on the role of a student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The game promises to offer an open-world experience, allowing players to explore the castle, grounds and Forbidden Forest, as well as attend classes and engage in magical duels. The game is set to be released in 2022 for PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4, Xbox Series X/S, Xbox One and PC.
